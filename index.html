Rooted in the land.

B's Bistro is a love letter to country living in the foothills of the Southern Drakensberg. Our cooking is honest, generous and seasonal, inspired by the farms and kitchens that surround Underberg.

Expect wood-fired mains, slow-braised classics, hand-rolled pastas and decadent desserts – plated with a light, modern touch and served with genuine rural hospitality.

The gentle march of our beloved ducks and chickens on their daily bedtime routine.

Seasonal & local

Weekly menus shaped by the weather, the garden and trusted local suppliers from the surrounding farms.

Comfort, elevated

Nostalgic flavours, refined plating. Think slow-roast lamb, buttery mash, garden greens and bold, elegant sauces.

Fire & warmth

Crackling fireplaces in winter, wide-open doors and golden fields in summer. A space designed for lingering.

The B's Bistro experience

Arrive to views of rolling pastures and towering trees, then step into a dining room of linen-dressed tables, soft candlelight and the aroma of something slow-roasting in the kitchen.

  • Thoughtful wine list with South African estates
  • Fireplace dining for crisp Underberg evenings
  • Sunlit veranda seating in summer
  • Small celebrations and intimate gatherings

Bernadene Marcus

Owner & Host

Born with a love for warm kitchens and long tables, Bernadene Marcus created B's Bistro as a place where locals and travellers can slow down, share generous food and feel genuinely looked after.

Rob Nelson

Head Chef

Rob Nelson leads the kitchen with a focus on soulful, fire-led cooking — balancing slow braises, perfect steaks and wood-fired sourdough pizzas with a refined, modern finish.
